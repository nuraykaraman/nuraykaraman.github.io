Welcome

I am a Visiting Assistant Professor of Sociology at Texas A&M University–Corpus Christi. Previously, I was an Associate Professor of Sociology at Uşak University in Turkey. My research focuses on race and ethnicity, gender, intersectionality and social theory, with particular attention to how social structures and institutions shape individuals’ lived experiences.

Interests

  • Gender
  • Intersectionality
  • Social Theory
